Place your licensed Tobias .woff2 files here (paths used in src/index.css):

  Tobias-Regular.woff2   → font-weight 400
  Tobias-Medium.woff2    → font-weight 500
  Tobias-SemiBold.woff2  → font-weight 600
  Tobias-Bold.woff2      → font-weight 700

If your export uses different names, either rename the files to match or edit the @font-face src URLs in src/index.css.
